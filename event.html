Attendees Rules & Regulations

Registration Rules

  • There is no refund for the registration once done.
  • If the registered candidate couldn't attend the event for any reason, refunds will not be processed.
  • Only one person may register at a time, and it is not transferable.
  • Registration cancellation and rescheduling are not permitted.
  • Attendees must certify that all information provided is accurate at the time of registration.
  • All registrations are subject to verification. If discrepancies are found, the organizing team may cancel the registration without refund.
  • Please bring a printout or show the ticket on your phone for verification at the venue.
  • A valid government-issued ID is required to collect your badge at the venue.

Safety & Security

  • Security checks will be in place. Kindly cooperate with police and private security.
  • The organizers reserve the right to stop and search any person entering the venue.
  • Illegal substances are strictly prohibited.
  • No food, drink, cigarettes, firearms, inflammables, or illegal items allowed.
  • The organizers are not responsible for injury, loss, or theft of personal property.
  • Attendees who misbehave may be asked to leave without a refund. This decision is final.
  • Attendees must follow all venue and general event guidelines.
